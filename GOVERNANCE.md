# Governance

## Maintainers
- [@Custos-com](https://github.com/Custos-com) — founder & lead maintainer

## How decisions are made
- Bug fixes and docs: merge after 1 review
- New features: open a GitHub Discussion first, then PR
- Breaking changes: maintainer decision + CHANGELOG entry

## How to become a maintainer
Contribute 3+ merged PRs and show up consistently in issues/discussions.
Open an issue titled "Maintainer nomination: your handle" and existing maintainers will vote.

## Long-term sustainability
Burnless is committed to remaining open source under Apache 2.0.
